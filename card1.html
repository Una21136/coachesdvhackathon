Prep 2

Recruit Allies

Ask the Players

Involve your community and inform them of the commitment your team is making. This will enhance your program’s success. We encourage you to work with the following audiences to increase school wide awareness and support for Coaching Boys into Men:

  • Parents

  • Principal

  • Athletic Director

Before the season begins, invite these allies to support you in the implementation of Coaching Boys into Men, and continue to involve them week after week. Share copies of the Coaching Boys into Men Overview, using it as a way to introduce anyone to the program.
